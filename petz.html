Story time

A couple was once driving down a dusty road. They hit a rabbit. The bunny was clearly deceased. It was sad.

After staring at the poor animal for a time they went to the trunk of the car and got a spray can.

They sprayed the rabbit and it slowly began to re-inflate and hop back to life.

Confused, nearby onlookers asked what was in the can.

They responded plainly, "hair restorer"
